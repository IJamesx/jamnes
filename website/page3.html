Firstly, cyber attacks can happen from misconfigured cloud instances which leads to vulnerabilities while opening doors for hackers in the process. Moreover, fake mobile apps can steal your data and even steal saved passwords on the devices. When there are massive data breaches in big companies, the staff are usually massively understaffed to deal with it while also keep their users calm. Lastly, a lot of apps use automatic updates which can lead to malware without the users knowledge.

Possible solutions

These are some potential solutions to help prevent a cyber attack:

1. Strengthen national cyber security sysem for rapid diagnosis and response to security incidents.

2. Develop a mechanism to certify all IT products for security assurance.

3. Launch formal cyber security education, awareness and skills programmes..
